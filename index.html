SK telecom

SK Telecom T1, commonly abbreviated as SKT, is a professional gaming team of the South
Korean telecommunications company SK Telecom. SKT's League of Legends team won the
2013, 2015, and 2016 League of Legends World Championships. The team has also won over 20
League of Legends tournaments including the 2015 and 2016 Mid-Season Invitational, the 2012
and 2016 IEM (Intel Extreme Masters)- World Championships, the 2015, 2016, and 2017 LCK
(League of Legends Champions Korea) Spring Playoffs, the 2015 LCK Summer Split and Playoffs,
All-Star Los Angeles 2015, and many more.
